MY BEAUTIFUL FAMILY

POTRAITS OF HOLIDAY

I spent most of my childhood and even now with my family. In addition to myself, I have four siblings. I'm the third in line. My older sister and brother are already married, while my younger brother and I are still in school. In Tumpat, Kelantan, our family lives happily prosperously. Since our family owns a business, we do most of our own work. Despite the fact that everyone is busy with their own work, we as a family still make time for each other, such as going on vacation together. I consider myself fortunate to have been born into a happy and harmonious family.
